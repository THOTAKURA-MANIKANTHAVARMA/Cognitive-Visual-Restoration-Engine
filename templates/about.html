🔬 Methodology

Data Collection

Curated a diverse dataset of over 1 million color images spanning various categories including landscapes, portraits, architecture, and nature. Images were preprocessed and converted to grayscale to create training pairs.

Model Architecture

Implemented a hybrid architecture combining U-Net for semantic segmentation and GANs for realistic color generation. The generator learns to predict color channels while the discriminator ensures photorealistic output.

Training Process

Models were trained on NVIDIA A100 GPUs for 200+ epochs using Adam optimizer with learning rate scheduling. Employed data augmentation techniques including rotation, scaling, and color jittering for robustness.

Evaluation Metrics

Performance evaluated using PSNR (Peak Signal-to-Noise Ratio), SSIM (Structural Similarity Index), and perceptual loss metrics. Achieved 28.5 dB PSNR and 0.92 SSIM on validation set.
